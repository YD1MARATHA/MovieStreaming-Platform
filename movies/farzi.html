Movie Description :
Farzi is about Sunny, an artist disillusioned by the income inequality in India after failing to keep his grandfather's extensive printing press in business. He decides to team up with his best friend Firoz to make counterfeit money. They face challenges from Mansoor, a gangster, and Michael, a cop.[1][2] According to co-creator Raj Nidimoru, the title Farzi (which means "fake") refers to counterfeiting, as well as the "fakeness" inherent in people.
